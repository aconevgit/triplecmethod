TEH SOLUTION

The Triple C Method™

Most client frameworks teach soft skills. The Triple C Method™ focuses on the three hard areas that actually determine profit and retention - Contract, Communication, and Calendar.
C1
Contract
The contract is the single source of truth for the relationship. Scope annex. Timelines. Milestones. What needs to be done? By which dates? Most teams sign it and ignore it until something breaks. You need to know it by heart.
— MSA & SOW.
C2
Communication
Step one: Limit how many people interact with the client. Step two: Appoint one person to own the relationship end to end, operationally. Step three: Set weekly documented meetings. What was said? What was promised? By whom?
— First what, then who.
C3
Calendar
Complex delivery takes months. Scope creep creates delays. But you can only validate a delay if you deviate from a schedule. Without one, you have no defense when the client demands faster. Keep a client-aligned schedule. Review it weekly.
— No schedule. No leverage.
