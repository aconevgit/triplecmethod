Asen Conev

The Triple C Method is not something I read about. It is what I built because I needed it — because I watched good work get destroyed by broken systems around it, and I decided to do something about it.

This is not project management theory. There are no frameworks borrowed from a textbook. Everything has been tested in real engagements, with real clients, under real pressure. It is simplified on purpose — because complexity is the enemy of execution.

If you are looking for an academic approach, this is not it. If you want something practical that works in your industry and your business, starting this week — keep reading.

The Company

Triple C Method

B2B Client-Facing Delivery

Triple C Method is built on a simple observation — most B2B service businesses over-invest in what they deliver and under-invest in how it is perceived.

The discipline we practise borrows from client psychology, delivery operations, and commercial strategy to close the gap between the value you create and the value your client actually feels. The Triple C Method is the operational foundation of that work — the delivery layer that makes everything else possible.

We work with a small number of clients at a time. That is by design. The work is specific, applied, and hands-on — not a course you buy and never finish.
